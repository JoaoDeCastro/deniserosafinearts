A Little Intro

It may sound cliché, but life is a journey, and we all want to have fun. Along the way we find some rocks on the road. The uncertainties, conflicts and worries fade out our playfulness and spirit of a child. As a Massage Therapist and face painter for over a decade, I understand the importance of awareness of reality without letting go of our inner child. As an artist my goal is to tell true or imaginative stories through my paintings, drawings or collages. My inner child allows me to create what and when I want according to my feelings. The diversity of medias and style let me go back and forth. It pushes me to be flexible as an artist and as a human being. Forms, colors, and texture are important elements of my constant process of learning. In my own journey I am still finding my pathway.
