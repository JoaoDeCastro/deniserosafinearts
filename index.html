Expositions

Exposition at Fabaic convention

Denise Rosa had the opportunity to showcase her artistic talent at the prestigious Fabaic Convention. Fabaic (Face and Body Art International Convention) is an internationally recognized event that brings together talented artists and enthusiasts from around the world. Denise's captivating artwork was featured in an exposition, where she captivated the audience with her unique style and storytelling abilities. Her intricate face painting designs and exceptional skills left a lasting impression on the attendees, solidifying her reputation as a remarkable artist in the field.

Danbury Fair Mall

In the Danbury Fair Mall, Denise Rosa's artwork focused on the theme of resurgence after the COVID-19 pandemic. Her paintings captured the essence of people overcoming their initial fears and hesitations about going out and gathering in parks and public spaces again. Denise's vibrant and uplifting artwork portrayed scenes of nature, parks, and communal activities that were temporarily put on hold during the pandemic. Through her paintings, she aimed to instill a sense of hope and encourage viewers to embrace the healing power of reconnecting with others and the beauty of the world around them. Her artwork served as a reminder of the resilience and unity that can triumph over adversity. By utilizing colors and textures that evoked warmth and comfort, Denise's paintings invited viewers to imagine a future filled with renewed enthusiasm and a rekindled appreciation for life's simple joys. Denise Rosa's art in the Danbury Fair Mall resonated with visitors, capturing the shared experience of navigating the uncertainties of the pandemic and emerging stronger together.

WCSU Senior exhibition

As a culmination of her academic journey, Denise Rosa was featured in the highly anticipated WCSU (Western Connecticut State University) Senior Exhibition. This exhibition serves as a platform for graduating art students to showcase their creative growth and accomplishments. Denise's body of work, comprising a diverse range of artistic mediums and styles, exemplified her artistic development and personal expression throughout her time at the university. Her artwork was thoughtfully curated, presenting a cohesive and compelling narrative that resonated with the viewers. The WCSU Senior Exhibition allowed Denise to receive recognition for her artistic talent and served as a springboard for her future endeavors in the art world.

Face Painting

With a gentle touch and a vivid imagination, Denise transforms the faces of children into magical creatures, superheroes, princesses, and more. Her artistic talent allows her to create intricate designs that capture the essence of each child's imagination and make their dreams come alive. Denise's experience in the field has granted her a deep understanding of children's preferences and interests. She engages with each child, ensuring a personalized and unforgettable face painting experience. Whether it's a whimsical butterfly, a ferocious tiger, or a dazzling unicorn, Denise's creations ignite smiles and spark the imagination of every young partygoer. Parents and party organizers have consistently praised Denise for her ability to create stunning face paintings while maintaining a safe and comfortable environment for children. Her attention to detail, use of high-quality, non-toxic paints, and adherence to hygiene practices ensure both the enjoyment and well-being of each child. Denise Rosa's dedication to her craft and her passion for bringing happiness to children's parties have made her a beloved and sought-after face painter in the community. Her ability to turn a simple face into a work of art has left a lasting impression on countless children and their families.

About Me

A Little Intro

Denise Rosa is an artist based in Connecticut, known for her exceptional talent in the art of face painting. With a profound passion for bringing joy and wonder to children, Denise's artistic journey began with face painting. For over a decade, she has been creating beautiful and imaginative designs on the faces of kids, transforming them into magical creatures, superheroes, and princesses.

Denise's artistic journey is rooted in her deep connection to art from a young age. Growing up, she discovered solace and self-expression through various artistic endeavors. However, it was through the enchanting world of face painting that she found her true calling.

With her gentle touch, vivid imagination, and years of experience, Denise has become a sought-after face painter for children's parties and events. Her ability to create intricate designs while engaging with each child on a personal level sets her apart. Denise's artistry not only captures the essence of a child's imagination but also fosters an environment of joy and creativity.

Denise Rosa's commitment to bringing smiles to the faces of children through her artistic talent has been the driving force behind her artistic journey. Through face painting, she continues to make a lasting impact, creating unforgettable moments of wonder and delight.
